Personal Bio.

I love all aspects of web development and I've been tinkering with HTML & CSS for a while now, before committing to a Full Stack Diploma with Code Institute. My goal is to transition into a new career in development as I find writing code to be a wonderful blend of creativity and logic. It is immensely satisfying work. I've also really started to appreciate how helpful and engaging the coding community is and how supportive and interested they are in new projects. Mostly I am excited to be increasing my programming knowledge and ability every day and I'm looking forward to expanding my development horizons further. This online resume was built as part of my Code Institute training and my hope is that it will help me to secure my first development job.

Skills.

I’m approaching software and web development from a background working in various creative industries. In many ways programming feels like a natural progression for me as it adds to and builds upon skills I have spent years developing. I've done quite a bit of graphic design, using the Adobe Suite, both as a design freelancer and for my own photography business and I've found those design skills to be invaluable when planning and wireframing this website. I initially discovered an interest in web development when I taught myself basic CSS in order to alter something on my photography business website. I was surprised then, at how creatively and intellectually engaging web development really is. I've listed some of my professional skills below, but a more complete outline can be found in my traditional CV, which is downloadable from the link in the footer of this website.

Photography

Photographic professional. Worked in industry in many capacities for over 10 years. Shooting, editing, professional retouching and advanced photography manipulation techniques.

Videography

Extensive experience planning, producing, shooting and editing professional videos for commercial use. Also skilled in using Premiere and After Effects for video editing and manipulation.

Design

Worked as a freelance designer for print media in the music, television and feature film industry. Both in Ireland and abroad. I have a good grasp of both Illustrator and InDesign.

UX/UI

I have been applying UX/UI practices to other creative work and have been studying it informally. I look forward to learning more and applying the concepts to my development work.
